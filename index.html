04 — Why

Because complexity is not the problem.

The absence of a sharper question is. Most organizations apply uniform force to every problem. This is efficient and consistently wrong.

A single precise question — asked before anyone else knows to ask it — produces results that no volume of effort applied afterward can replicate. We exist for that question.

06 — Method

Less process.
More precision.

We start with a brief, not a deck. We diagnose before we prescribe. We design for the outcome, not the approval. We know when to stop — because overworked work shows.

Three principles. Problems are solved at the level of structure, not surface. Every deliverable should be able to explain itself without us in the room. The best work looks inevitable in retrospect and impossible to have predicted in advance.

07 — AI

We use it.
We declare it.

We are not impressed by AI — but we are not afraid of it either. AI is a multiplier, not a replacement. We use language models to compress time on structured tasks: research, drafting, pattern recognition, data transformation.

Every AI-assisted output is declared.
Transparency is a design principle,
not just a legal one.
